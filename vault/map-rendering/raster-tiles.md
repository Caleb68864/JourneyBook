# Raster Tiles

Raster tiles are pre-rendered images, usually PNG/JPEG/WebP, served by zoom/x/y. They are simple to display and stitch, but labels are baked in and can blur or clip in print. For Journey Book they are acceptable for prototypes, but vector or local rendering gives better print control.
