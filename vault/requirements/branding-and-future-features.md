# Branding and Future Features

Journey Book is broad enough to support more than maps. The brand can grow into hiking journals, road-trip activities, scout practice, state park adventures, field trips, historical tours, and geocaching.

The visual direction should lean toward field notebooks, national park signs, scout handbooks, vintage adventure books, and old road atlases. Avoid military or enterprise GIS styling.

Future modules can include Journey Atlas, Landmark Finder, Compass Lessons, Explorer Log, Journey Challenges, and Print Center.
