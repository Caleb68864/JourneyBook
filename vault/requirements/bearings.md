# Bearings

Bearings are angle directions measured in degrees. They are useful for older kids and outdoor routes, but should not be required for a road atlas MVP. Add bearing exercises after compass orientation and scale lessons are clear.

TC 3-25.26 uses the military term azimuth for a horizontal direction expressed as an angle. Journey Book can use "bearing" in kid-facing lessons and "azimuth" in advanced land-nav mode.
