# Navigator Challenge Pages

Navigator challenges are themed activity sheets tied to the generated atlas. A school-route atlas might ask: find the railroad crossing, find the river, determine travel direction, and estimate distance to school.

Advanced challenge pages can borrow the TC 3-25.26 progression: read marginal information, identify map symbols, find a grid square, measure distance, determine direction, interpret contours, and plan a route.
