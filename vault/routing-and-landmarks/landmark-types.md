# Landmark Types

Best landmark candidates: schools, churches, parks, water towers, railroads, bridges, rivers, lakes, fire stations, hospitals, grain elevators, courthouses, libraries, trailheads, and major road intersections. Avoid temporary businesses as primary teaching anchors.
