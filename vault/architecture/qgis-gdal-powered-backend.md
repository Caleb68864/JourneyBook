# QGIS GDAL Powered Backend

Use QGIS/GDAL automation for professional cartographic layouts. Pros: mature GIS tooling and layout concepts. Cons: huge dependency footprint and GPL considerations. Best for internal prototypes or power-user mode. Pitfalls: hard packaging and slow startup.
