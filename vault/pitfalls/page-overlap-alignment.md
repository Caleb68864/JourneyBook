# Page Overlap Alignment

## Risk
Overlaps can be inconsistent or unlabeled.

## Why It Matters
Users may think duplicate roads are a mistake.

## Mitigation
Use a fixed overlap percentage and mark overlap subtly.

## Prototype Check
Compare adjacent pages side by side.
