# Missing Attribution

## Risk
Source credits can be omitted or clipped.

## Why It Matters
Missing attribution creates legal and trust problems.

## Mitigation
Generate attribution from active layers and place it inside safe margins on every relevant page.

## Prototype Check
Add a test that every map page contains attribution text.
