# Too Much Detail For Kids

## Risk
Professional topo detail can distract from the lesson.

## Why It Matters
Kids need recognizable features and clear tasks.

## Mitigation
Rank and filter landmarks. Offer beginner/advanced detail modes.

## Prototype Check
Show the same page to a parent and ask what a child should notice first.
