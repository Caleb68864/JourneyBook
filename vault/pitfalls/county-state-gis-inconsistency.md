# County State GIS Inconsistency

## Risk
Local GIS data varies wildly in schema, license, freshness, and availability.

## Why It Matters
A pipeline that works in one county may fail in the next.

## Mitigation
Treat local GIS as optional enhancement, not MVP dependency.

## Prototype Check
Try three counties and record required adapters.
