# Contour Line Readability

## Risk
Contours can overwhelm young readers or vanish in print.

## Why It Matters
Terrain education fails if lines are too dense or faint.

## Mitigation
Use scale-aware contour intervals and optional simplified terrain mode.

## Prototype Check
Print the hilliest expected page and inspect through a page protector.
