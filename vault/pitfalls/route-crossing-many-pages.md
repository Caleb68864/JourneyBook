# Route Crossing Many Pages

## Risk
Long routes can create many page transitions.

## Why It Matters
Kids may lose the route across sheets.

## Mitigation
Add overview, route segment index, and route-specific continuation labels.

## Prototype Check
Generate a long skinny route and review page order.
