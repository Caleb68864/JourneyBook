# Poor Landmark Selection

## Risk
The app may choose invisible, temporary, or boring landmarks.

## Why It Matters
Bad landmarks make challenges feel arbitrary.

## Mitigation
Prefer durable visible features and diversify by page.

## Prototype Check
Manually review top 20 selected landmarks for a sample route.
