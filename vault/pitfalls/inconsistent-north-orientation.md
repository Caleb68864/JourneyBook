# Inconsistent North Orientation

## Risk
Rotated pages make the atlas hard to follow.

## Why It Matters
Neighbor references and compass practice depend on stable north-up pages.

## Mitigation
Keep detailed maps north-up and route arrows independent.

## Prototype Check
Check page transforms and compass rose orientation.
