# USGS National Map

## What It Provides
U.S. national map layers including elevation, hydrography, boundaries, transportation, structures, land cover, imagery, and topo basemaps.

## API Type
Download products plus REST, WMS, WMTS, WFS, and WCS services depending on layer.

## Limits and Access
No simple universal quota found in the official pages. Treat services as public infrastructure and cache/download responsibly.

## Licensing
The National Map data and services are free and public domain, with USGS requesting source acknowledgment.

## Attribution
Use the requested wording: 'Data available from U.S. Geological Survey, National Geospatial Program.'

## Commercial Use
Allowed. No restrictions are stated for downloaded data, but source credit is requested.

## Offline or Cache Use
Good offline fit when data is downloaded. Service-tile caching should still respect service behavior and avoid abusive bulk requests.

## Best Use In Journey Book
Best U.S. source for topographic context, contours, hydrography, elevation, and public-domain map layers.

## Pitfalls
U.S.-only, layer complexity, and cartographic styling may need simplification for kids.
